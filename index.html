The Fastest Hero in the DC Universe

Flash: the iconic scarlet speedster from DC Comics. Known as Barry Allen, he is a forensic scientist who, after being struck by lightning and exposed to chemicals, gained extraordinary powers. With his ability to run at superhuman speeds, the Flash protects Central City and the world from unimaginable threats. Follow his thrilling adventures as he faces formidable villains and inspires hope in all of us.
